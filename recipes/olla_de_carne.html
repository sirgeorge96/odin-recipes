Meat Pot

The meat pot is a Costa Rican stew whose variety of flavors and textures, exquisiteness and nutritional power make it a traditional dish that usually brings together families and friends at lunchtime.

It consists of a broth with pieces of meat and vegetables seasoned with flavors and aromas, whose cooking requires a careful procedure over low heat, to give the ingredients a smooth and uniform texture.

The preferred meat is cecina, shank and beef rib and the variety of vegetables can be as wide as they want.

    Ingredients
  • Potato
  • Corn
  • Cow meat(cecina)
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Squash
  • Sweet Chili
    Steps to prepare
  1. Put the meat covered with water in a large pot
  2. Add the spices ( sweet chili, onion, coriander, celery) and cook over low heat.
  3. Add the salt and the vegetables, these from more consistent to less consistent (to prevent the softer ones from falling apart)
  4. When they are very soft, serve with white rice and tortillas.
